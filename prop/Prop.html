What is a Prop?



A prop is a type of content that you can create and upload to ChilloutVR, A prop can be anything from a chair to a gun, as long as it follows the content guidelines and rules of ChilloutVR.

You can use props to customize your avatar, your world, or your game mode. Props can also have animations and sounds attached to them, making them more interactive and fun.

To create a prop, you need to use the ChilloutVR Content Creation Kit (CCK), which is a Unity-based tool that allows you to import your own models, textures, and sounds, and set up the properties and behaviors of your prop. You can then upload your prop to the ChilloutVR cloud and use it in the game.

You can also share your prop with other players and join the ChilloutVR community of content creators.

 

Safety and Content Control

While ChilloutVR offers a wide range of experiences, it is important to note that some Props may contain content that is not suitable for all audiences.

The platform provides options for users to toggle NSFW (not safe for work) content on or off, allowing individuals to customize their experience based on their preferences.

It is essential to exercise caution and adhere to community guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for all users.

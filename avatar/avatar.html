What is a Avatar?

 

An avatar is a virtual representation of yourself in ChilloutVR, a sandbox game playable in VR & Desktop. You can create, customize and upload your own avatars using the Unity-based content creation kit provided by the game developers. You can also download and use avatars created by other players from the community.

 

Avatars are more than just 3D models. They can have animations, sounds, expressions, gestures and interactions that make them unique and immersive. You can also switch between different avatars in the game depending on your mood and preference.

 

To create an avatar for ChilloutVR, you need to have a basic knowledge of Unity and how to set up a humanoid rig for your model. You also need to add a CVR Avatar Descriptor component to your avatar, which allows you to configure various settings and features for your avatar. You can find more details and tutorials on how to use the content creation kit on the official website.

 

Creating an avatar for ChilloutVR is a fun and rewarding way to express yourself and share your creativity with others. You can also join groups and events with other players who share your interests and passions. ChilloutVR is a place where you can be yourself and enjoy endless worlds and experiences with your friends.

 

Safety and Content Control

While ChilloutVR offers a wide range of experiences, it is important to note that some Avatars may contain content that is not suitable for all audiences.

The platform provides options for users to toggle NSFW (not safe for work) content on or off, allowing individuals to customize their experience based on their preferences.

It is essential to exercise caution and adhere to community guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for all users.

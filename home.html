ChilloutVR


ChilloutVR is a social VR platform that allows users to create and share their own virtual reality experiences with others. It was founded in 2019 by a team of VR enthusiasts who wanted to create a platform that was easy to use and accessible to everyone.

ChilloutVR offers a wide range of features that allow users to create their own virtual worlds, games, and experiences. Users can create custom avatars, import 3D models, and use a variety of tools to build their own environments.

One of the unique features of ChilloutVR is its focus on social interaction. Users can interact with each other in real-time using voice chat and text messaging. They can also attend events and meetups hosted by other users.

ChilloutVR is available on a variety of VR platforms, including Valve Index, Oculus Rift, HTC Vive, and Windows Mixed Reality headsets.
